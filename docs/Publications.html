Title 4: Building Interoperability between Enterprise Architecture IT Portfolio Management and Visual Modeling Platform

Author: Dr Nicolas Figay

Reference: https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/introduction-dr-nicolas-figay-hdr-sucqe/

Summary: I recently participated in a pilot project to integrate two platforms: SoftwareAG’s Alfabet for enterprise architecture and IT management, and Sparx Enterprise Architect with ProCloud Server for collaborative modeling and standardized ArchiMate architecture documentation. This project provided a real-world case of enterprise interoperability, aligning with the theoretical approach I've developed in my research. It also highlighted new aspects to enhance my framework. In this article, I outline the project, its challenges, and key lessons learned.
